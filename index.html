Project highlights

  • Steampy Steam wish-list price drop notifications by SMS

    Steampy is a notification service for your Steam wishlist. All you need to do is sign up, set the threshold and your phone number. We'll track your wishlist items for sales and discounts. As soon as the price drops, you'll get a text message with the name, price and a link to the store. You'll never miss your favourite game on sale again.

  • S2048 2048 game with Skype emoticons

    Clone of 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
